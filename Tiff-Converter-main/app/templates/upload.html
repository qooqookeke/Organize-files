This open-source online tool transforms TIFF files into optimized JPG/PNG. It can handle files of upto 50 MB.

  1. Click on Choose Files, and choose TIFF files upto 50 MB that you want to convert into JPG / PNG format.
  2. Choose the output file extension (jpg / png).
  3. Then, wait for the conversion to complete. Download your image files, in a single ZIP folder.
